Introduction

A portfolio built to showcase work, not to harvest data.

This website is operated by Temidayo Abraham Durodola as a professional portfolio for industrial design and automotive engineering work. It does not operate user accounts, e-commerce functionality or behavioural advertising.

By browsing this site or making contact through it, a visitor agrees to the practices described in this policy. Anyone who does not agree with these terms should discontinue use of the site.

Information We Collect

What is collected, and when.

Provided Directly

Direct Correspondence

When a visitor reaches out by email regarding a portfolio enquiry, collaboration or project, the name, email address and message content shared are received and retained as part of that correspondence.

Collected Automatically

Standard Technical Data

As with most websites, the hosting infrastructure automatically logs basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type and pages visited, for security and performance purposes.

How Information Is Used

Used to respond and to keep the site running, nothing more.

Responding to Enquiries

Information shared by email is used solely to reply to messages and discuss potential projects or collaboration.

Maintaining Site Performance

Automatically logged technical data is used only to monitor uptime, diagnose issues and protect the site against abuse.

No Marketing Communications

Information provided through correspondence is never used to send unsolicited marketing emails or added to a mailing list.

No Sale or Sharing

Personal information is never sold, rented or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies.

No cookies are set for advertising, profiling or cross-site tracking purposes. The site does not currently use cookie-based analytics or marketing tools.

Typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts as part of the site's design. Google Fonts is requested without the use of cookies, though the request itself may transmit the visitor's IP address to Google as a normal function of loading the font files.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards, applied consistently.

This site is served over an encrypted HTTPS connection, and correspondence is handled through standard, reputable email infrastructure. No separate database of visitor personal information is maintained on this site.

Information is retained only as long as necessary to respond to an enquiry or fulfil the purpose for which it was shared, after which it is deleted or archived as part of ordinary email housekeeping.

This site is accessible globally and served through a distributed hosting network, which means information may be processed in a country other than the visitor's own as a normal function of how the internet works.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While reasonable precautions are taken, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
